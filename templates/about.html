Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis, also known as opinion mining is the process of classifying the sentiment whether a block of text is positive, negative or neutral conveyed within a piece of text. As the internet and social media platforms continue to serve as large number of mediums for communication, the analysis of user genereted content has gained supreme importance. Individuals, business and organisations seek to extract valuable insights from vast datasets comprising customer reviews, social media posts, and textual feedback.
